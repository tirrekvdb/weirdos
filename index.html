How was this NFT collection built?
Each Steampunk AI Mutant is based on a generated image,
then transformed via AI into up to 4 subsets of uniqe art styles.
Each art style has been iterated up to 80 times.
The metadata reflects these informations:
    Base Image: Number of base image (1-1000)
    Art Style: one of 22 Art Styles
    Level: Number of iterations (1=20, 2=40, 3=60 and 4=80 iterations)
    Steampunk Weight: (how strong is the Steampunk theme)
    Subset: Number of Subset (up to 4 different art styles per base image)
The rarest art style is 4%
There will be 10 hidden clips of the mutations as super rare items.
